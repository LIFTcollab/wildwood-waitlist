# LIFT Collaborative — Strategic Context

## About LIFT Collaborative

LIFT Collaborative is a nonprofit focused on human-centered design and innovation. The acronym stands for **Let's Innovate the Future Together**.

LIFT operates as a **backbone organization** that orchestrates multi-sector partnerships around shared causes. The model is sector-agnostic, though each partnership is cause-specific.

## The Head, Heart, Hands Framework

LIFT's operating philosophy uses the metaphor of Head, Heart, and Hands to represent three modalities of the work:

- **Head** — Intention and focus
- **Heart** — Compassion and empathy
- **Hands** — Connection of people to people and people to ideas

### How This Manifests in Practice

**Head (Intention and Focus)**
- Clarity of purpose for every program and partnership
- Rigorous theory of change
- Evidence-based approach
- Strategic frameworks that connect work across partnerships

**Heart (Compassion and Empathy)**
- Human-centered design — co-designing *with* communities, not *for* them
- Stories of real people centered in all communications
- Warm, direct, jargon-free language
- Design choices that feel human, not corporate

**Hands (Connection)**
- Building ecosystems, not just programs
- Visible network and partnership maps
- Clear pathways for collaboration
- Action-oriented platform tools

## The Platform Vision

LIFT is building a platform that provides nonprofits with purpose-built tools, designed in partnership with them. The platform serves two strategic purposes:

1. **Operational** — Tools that solve real administrative burden for nonprofits
2. **Strategic** — Infrastructure for multi-sector partnerships around shared causes

The platform is positioned as **collective impact infrastructure** — the backbone that allows multiple partners to align around a shared cause with measurable outcomes.

## Funder Positioning

### Primary Audience
Potential funders — businesses and foundations that would sponsor multi-sector partnerships with a common cause.

### The Messaging Sequence for Funders
**Lead with Heart, validate with Head, close with Hands**:

- **Heart** earns attention — *"Here's the human problem worth solving"*
- **Head** earns trust — *"Here's the rigorous strategy behind our approach"*
- **Hands** earns commitment — *"Here's the ecosystem you're joining, not just funding"*

### The Unique Funder Proposition
Most foundations fund vertically — one cause, one sector. LIFT offers something rarer:

> A neutral convening infrastructure where business, government, philanthropy, and community organizations align around a shared cause — with LIFT as the trusted backbone.

### Funder Personas

| Funder Type | What They're Buying | How to Frame LIFT |
|---|---|---|
| Corporate Sponsor | Brand alignment + employee engagement | "Join a cause coalition that reflects your values" |
| Community Foundation | Local systems change | "We build the table everyone sits at" |
| National Foundation | Replicable model + scale | "Proven infrastructure, adaptable to any cause" |
| Government/Public Sector | Accountability + reach | "Multi-sector accountability with measurable outcomes" |

### The Sector-Agnostic Tension
LIFT's mission is sector-agnostic. Each partnership is cause-specific. This must be communicated clearly to funders who organize giving around causes.

## The Child Care Beachhead — Colorado

While the platform is sector-agnostic, the **first strategic focus** is child care in Colorado:

- Child care in Colorado is a critical, underfunded system
- Providers lack shared infrastructure
- Directors drown in administration
- Funders care deeply but struggle to find scalable intervention points
- Broad funder coalition exists — corporate, foundation, government, employer

### The Phased Vision

| Phase | What It Looks Like |
|---|---|
| Now | Wildwood waitlist app — one school, one problem solved |
| Near | Five child care providers on platform — shared infrastructure |
| Medium | Statewide child care network — waitlists, enrollment, family communication |
| Long | LIFT as backbone for child care systems change — state and national scale |

## The Wildwood Origin Story

**Wildwood Preschool** is LIFT's first nonprofit partner and the platform's proof of concept.

- The director was spending hours manually managing a multi-year waitlist
- LIFT co-designed a waitlist management app with the director
- Result: time savings and better information around enrollment
- Lives at `wildwood.liftcollab.app`

### Why Wildwood Matters Strategically

It hits all three modalities:
- **Heart** — Children, families, early childhood. Universal resonance with funders.
- **Head** — Early childhood is one of the most evidence-backed intervention points
- **Hands** — Preschools sit at the intersection of education, family services, healthcare, and community

It's a **founding story**, not just a feature:

> "We didn't start with a grand platform. We started with one preschool, one community, and a belief that the right tools — built with care — could multiply their impact."
